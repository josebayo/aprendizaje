Basic calculator

Functioning of the calculator

  • Press 1 to add
  • Press 2 to subtract
  • Press 3 to multiply
  • Press 4 to split
  • Press 5 to exit

Depending on the selected option, it will carry out the operation and if you press option 6, with the confirm button, ask if you are sure to exit, if it is, if it will close the window, if not, an option from the menu will be chosen again.
